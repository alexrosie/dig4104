For a child or adult with disabilities, therapeutic horseback riding is much more than riding around an arena. It’s a way to discover a whole new world of adventure, freedom, responsibility, dignity and excitement. Most importantly, it provides an overwhelming sense of accomplishment for everyone involved – riders, volunteers, parents, and sponsors.

Whether you choose to sponsor a rider, sponsor a horse, or donate in general, be assured that your contribution will touch many lives.

Any amount, large or small, is a gift of freedom. THANK YOU!
