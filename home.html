Welcome to Freedom Ride, a non-profit 501(c)3 volunteer-based organization built on the conviction that we are not bound by our physical limitations. Freedom Ride is dedicated to enriching the lives and experiences of children and adults with disabilities through therapeutic horseback riding with the generous help of our volunteers.
