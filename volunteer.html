Freedom Ride Volunteers are able to be a part of all aspects of our program. Volunteers assist with cleaning stalls, grooming the horses, helping our students in their lessons, fundraisers, special events, etc. No horse experience is required, but all Volunteers should be comfortable working with and around them.

We ask our Volunteers to commit to a few hours a week for each session. Our sessions generally run January-May, June-August, and September-December. Volunteer shifts are 7 days a week, mornings, afternoons and evenings depending on the schedule of that day.

Groups

Individuals

  1. Must be at least 14 years old
  2. Download the Volunteer Registration Forms and the Letter of Recommendation
  3. Complete and return your forms to Freedom Ride
  4. You will receive an email invitation to sign up for orientation (there will be a non-refundable $20 registration fee due at orientation)
